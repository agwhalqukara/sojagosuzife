    04 Aug 2024, 00:00

    We recently let Taken – made up of members Ste Baron, Eoghan Mac Mahon, and Richie Power – take over our official Twitter account. The trio have carried out many headline shows across the UK, Ireland, and Europe. Taken have recently finished supporting The Vamps on their ‘Up Close and Personal Tour’. The band recently debuted the emotional and powerful music video for their new single ‘Losing Game’, which you can watch below:

    Here at CelebMix, we review five things we learned from Taken’s Twitter Takeover.

    1. Eoghan revealed how long it took to write ‘Losing Game’ – no doubt a lot of hard work carried out behind-the-scenes went into the track!

    I would say about 6 hours worth of sessions to write it and then lots of more in the studio doing the production ? – Eoghan #CMTaken

    — CelebMix (@CelebMix) August 14, 2017

    2. Ste revealed what he’s most looking forward to when the band head out on their headline tour later this year!

    Meeting some crazy people and playing to some amazing people. You? – Ste #CMTaken

    — CelebMix (@CelebMix) August 14, 2017

    3. The band wasn’t expecting the overwhelming response they received for ‘Losing Game.’

    Seriously unreal! Wasn't expecting it! What you think of the video? Richie #CMTaken

    — CelebMix (@CelebMix) August 14, 2017

    4. Richie debuted a hidden talent – doing tongue twisters?!

    #CMTaken – Richie pic.twitter.com/X02btHgnRS
