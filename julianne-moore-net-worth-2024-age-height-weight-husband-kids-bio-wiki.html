    Early life

    The American actress was born as Julie Anna Smith on December 3, 1960, in Fayetteville, North Caroline, US. Her mother, Anne, was a social worker while her father, Peter Moore Smith, was an army officer at that time. So Julianne’s childhood was spent in moving city to city. Due to the fact, she also had to change her school many times.

    As a child, Julianne travelled to different locations around the world with her parents. She completed her high school from Frankfurt American High School in Germany. Finally, her family moved to the US just after her schools. Here Julianne enrolled at Boston University and graduated with a degree in acting and theatre.
