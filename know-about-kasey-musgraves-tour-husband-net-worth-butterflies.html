    Kasey Musgrave is a famous American singer and songwriter known for pop and country music. She is the recipient of six Grammy Awards; two in the year 2014 and four in 2019. Before a proper musical career, she was a part of the fifth season of the USA Network’s singing show Nashville Star in 2007.

    Kasey Musgraves Bio & Family

    The singer was born Kacey Lee Musgraves on 21 August 1988 in Golden, Texas. Her parents, Craig, and Karen (née Abrams) Musgraves raised her and her younger sister, Kelly Christine Sutton (née Musgraves), who is into photography.

    Their father owns a small printing business, M-Prints Printing, Inc., in Carthage, Texas, while their mother, Karen is an artist. She went to Mineola High School to complete her graduation in 2006 and then shifted to Austin, Texas at the age of 18. She stands at the height of (5 ft 5 in) 165 cm and weighs around 60 kg.
