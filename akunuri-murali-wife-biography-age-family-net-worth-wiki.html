    Who Is Akunuri Murali? - Akunuri Murali Wikipedia

    Akunuri Murali Wikipedia: - Akunuri Murali was born in Warangal, Telangana in 1968. Akunuri Murali is a former Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer who served in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh states. He is from the Scheduled Caste community and experienced discrimination and suffering as a youngster. He finished his education at a government school before enrolling in Osmania University's Bachelor of Engineering program. He then earned a Master of Technology degree from the National Institute of Technology, Warangal. He also has a Public Administration certificate from the Indian Institute of Public Administration in New Delhi.

    Murali joined the Indian Administrative Service in 2006 and was assigned to the Telangana cadre. He served as joint collector of Ranga Reddy district, collector of Jayashankar Bhupalapally district, commissioner of archives and research center, and director of tribal welfare, among other positions. He was well-known for his forward-thinking and people-centered efforts in education, health, agriculture, and government. He also worked as a consultant for the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP).
