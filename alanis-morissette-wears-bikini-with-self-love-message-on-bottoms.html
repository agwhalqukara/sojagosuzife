    You oughtta know, Alanis Morissette has nothing to hide. The Grammy-winning singer was spotted on Saturday, May 4, enjoying the sand and surf in a revealing mismatched two-piece — a striped black and white string top paired with low-rise black bottoms — while vacationing with her son Ever and husband Mario “MC Souleye” Treadway in Hawaii.     Morissette, 38, wearing no makeup, seemed content and happy as she watched her towhead tot, 2, playing on the beach. On the back of her bikini bottom bore a message that seemed in line with her serene demeanor: “Self Love.”
