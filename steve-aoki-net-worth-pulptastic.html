    What is Steve Aoki’s Net Worth?

    Steve Aoki is a successful American electro house musician, DJ, and record producer. He is also a music executive and the founder of Dim Mak Records. Aoki has achieved great success in his career, and his net worth is estimated to be $120 million, making him one of the wealthiest musicians in the world.

    Throughout his career, Aoki has collaborated with many notable artists, including Linkin Park, Iggy Azalea, Lil Jon, blink-182, BTS, Backstreet Boys, Rise Against, and Fall Out Boy. He has also been recognized as the highest-grossing dance artist in North America on several occasions.

    Early Life and Education

    Steven Hiroyuki Aoki was born on November 30, 1977, in Miami, Florida to Rocky Aoki and Chizuru Kobayashi. His father is a former Japanese wrestler who founded the restaurant chain Benihana. Aoki has two older siblings, sister Kana and brother Kevin, as well as half-sister Devon Aoki. When Aoki was a child, his family relocated to Newport Beach, California. He graduated from Newport Harbor High School in 1995 and went to college at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

    At college, Aoki graduated with two BA degrees – one in Feminist Studies and the other in Sociology. During his time at the Santa Barbara Student Housing Cooperative where he lived, he ran underground concerts.

    Steve Aoki: DJ, Record Label Founder, and Clothing Designer

    Steve Aoki is a popular DJ who established a following on college campuses by touring extensively. He performs about 250 shows annually. Aoki founded his own record label, Dim Mak Records, in 1996, which he named after Bruce Lee, his childhood hero. In addition to being a DJ, Aoki is a club promoter and has remixed many well-known artists and bands. His debut studio album, “Wonderland,” was released in 2012, and he has since released several more albums, including “Neon Future I,” “Neon Future II,” “Steve Aoki Presents Kolony,” and “Neon Future III.”

    Aoki also has his own clothing line, The Dim Mak Collection. In 2020, he released his latest album, “Neon Future IV,” and launched a YouTube show called “The Neon Future Dinner Series” in response to the Covid-19 pandemic. The show features Aoki and various guests discussing topics ranging from music to social issues. Among the guests have been Tony Hawk, Shaun White, T-Pain, will.i.am, Lisa Ling, Ross Butler, Henry Golding, and Michelle Phan. “The Neon Future Dinner Series” is available on Aoki’s YouTube channel.

    Steve Aoki’s Personal Life and Charity Work

    In 2015, Steve Aoki married his long-time girlfriend, Tiernan Cowling, in Hawaii. She is Australian. They were engaged for five years.

    Aoki is a gamer. In 2020, he participated in the Warzone Royal Ruckus, a charity tournament of the best gamers, athletes, musicians, and influencers playing Call of Duty in support of the Equal Justice Initiative. The organization is committed to ending mass incarceration and excessive punishment in the United States, challenging racial and economic injustice, and protecting basic human rights for the most vulnerable people in American society.

    More than 100 pro gamers and celebs came together to compete in Call of Duty: Warzone Royal Ruckus two day charity tournament on June 11 and 12, 2020.

    Aoki Foundation Supports Brain Research and Other Charities

    The Aoki Foundation focuses on funding research related to the brain, with an emphasis on regenerative medicine and brain preservation. Additionally, the foundation supports disaster relief efforts, animal rights, and people with developmental disabilities.

    During his 2013 North American tour, Steve Aoki donated a portion of ticket sales to four charities: the Brain Preservation Foundation, the University of Rochester Medical Center – Memory Care Program, the American Brain Foundation, and Infusio. Fans were asked to vote for their preferred charity, with the Brain Preservation Foundation receiving $33,000 of the $65,000 raised.

    Steve Aoki’s Technologically Savvy Home in Las Vegas

    Steve Aoki and his wife reside in a 16,000 square foot home in Henderson, Nevada. The property was built in 2008 at the height of the global financial crisis and real estate market meltdown and was on the market for $12 million for years.

    In 2014, the property went up for auction, and Aoki purchased it for less than $3 million, but he had to pay all cash. Aoki spent years and millions of dollars to convert the property into a technologically savvy and modernist space, which he calls Aoki’s Playhouse.

    Steve Aoki’s Art Collection

    Steve Aoki has an impressive art collection that includes pieces by renowned artists Damien Hirst and Banksy.
